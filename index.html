Welcome to My Website

Hi, I'm Nora Zajzon, a Computer Science student at the University of California, San Diego, with a minor in Cognitive Science. I have a strong passion for software engineering, particularly in front-end and back-end development, as well as research in brain-computer interfaces. I have experience working with React, Node.js, Python, C++, C, Javascript, and Java, and I enjoy building intuitive and efficient web applications. My work spans software engineering internships, open-source contributions, and academic research, including projects on EEG-based handwriting detection and machine learning applications in neuroscience. I am also an active member of Women in Computing at UCSD, where I contribute to technical development projects and support diversity in tech. Currently, I'm working on innovative web applications and researching EEG-based handwriting detection. Feel free to check out my projects, research, or to reach out.
